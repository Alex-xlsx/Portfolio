More About Alex

 My whole life I thought coding was something only the smartest people, who had been working with it since high school, could do. General Assembly has taught me that anyone can code, and ever since I have been excitedly digging in. While I may not have been coding for long, I have an avid desire to push how far I can go.

 The other newest skill I've been sharpening is digital painting. While it may not be fair to completely call myself a novice- I've been drawing and dabbling in art since I was small -I am still new to painting with this medium. Quarantine gave me an excellent opportunity to fast track my skills with it, and now its one of my favorite past-times.

 Photography is something I hadn't really tried to do, so much as had it fall into my lap. I took a single class in it while attending college, and offered to help a friend with some photography they needed; next thing I knew, I was suddenly getting requests from all my friends, and then from others they had recommended me to. While it was unexpected for me, I really enjoy it.
